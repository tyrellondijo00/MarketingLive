Our History


SmartVision was founded in 2011 by a group of Somali-based development professionals in response to the lack of local firms providing professional support to humanitarian agencies operating in Somalia. Since its inception, SmartVision has continued to strengthen its ranks which now include a strong team of both local and international staff, and with offices across the whole of Somalia. In our growth process, the organisation has archieved the following milestones:

2013

  • The company recruited a pool of international staff from different parts of East Africa and Europe and opened a satelite office in Nairobi

2016

  • Smart Vision opened its second office in Mogadishu and formed a research team to take care of South-central Somalia

2019

  • The firm opened a third office in Hargesia to take care of its operations in Somaliland and Somali region of Ethiopia

2021

  • Smart Vision established its satellite offices in Baidoa to coordinate operations in South West State

Our Background


Smart Vision was established in response to an overwhelming need among humanitarian and development organizations for efficient and accurate monitoring, evaluation, and research services. We are driven by a passion to help our clients reach their maximum potential in Somalia and the Horn of Africa. Since our inception 10 years ago, Smart Vision has become an industry leader in Somalia and Somaliland.
